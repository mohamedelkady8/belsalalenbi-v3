About Us

Where did the name "Alsalat Ala Alnaby" come from?
Our father used to own a large fish market in Ismailia. Whenever a new fishing boat arrived or a fresh fish batch was unloaded, we would say “Yalla, bless the Prophet” – in Arabic: “Yalla belsalat ‘ala elnaby” – as a sign of good fortune. That’s where the name was born.

The first branch was opened in Ismailia in 1995 at the local fish market near Al-Usra beach. Over time, it became one of the top seafood restaurants in the Canal cities. In 2019, the branch relocated to its current location in front of Al-Nawras Village. In 2024, we expanded with a second branch in New Cairo – Fifth Settlement.
